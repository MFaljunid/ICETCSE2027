The Conference

About ICETCSE-2027

ICETCSE-2027 (International Conference on Emerging Trends in Computer Science & Engineering) is organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Moodlakatte Institute of Technology Kundapura (MITK), Karnataka, India.

The conference aims to bring together leading researchers, engineers, and practitioners from academia and industry worldwide to exchange experiences and research results on all aspects of Computer Science and Engineering.

All accepted and presented papers will be published in Elsevier Procedia Computer Science, indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, and ScienceDirect — ensuring global visibility for your research.

The Institution

About MITK

Moodlakatte Institute of Technology Kundapura (MITK) is a reputed engineering institution located in the coastal town of Kundapura, Udupi District, Karnataka, India. The institution is committed to excellence in technical education, research, and innovation.

📍 Location

Kundapura, Udupi District, Karnataka — 576 201. Nestled in the beautiful coastal belt of Karnataka.

🎓 Programs

Undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering and Technology, accredited by AICTE.

🔬 Research

Active research groups in AI, IoT, Cybersecurity, and Data Science with industry collaborations.

🌐 Connectivity

Well connected to Udupi (50 km), Mangaluru (80 km), and accessible by road, rail, and air.
