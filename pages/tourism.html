Explore Kundapura

Extend your visit and explore the stunning coastal Karnataka region.

📍 5 km from MITK

🏖️ Maravanthe Beach

Sea on both sides of the road — one of the most unique beaches in India. Perfect sunset spot.

📍 40 km from MITK

🛕 Kollur Mookambika Temple

Sacred temple of Goddess Mookambika amid lush Western Ghats — a must-visit pilgrimage site.

📍 50 km from MITK

⛩️ Udupi Sri Krishna Temple

One of the most revered temples in South India with centuries of history and culture.

📍 60 km from MITK

🏝️ St. Mary's Island

Unique hexagonal basalt rock formations and pristine white sand beaches. Accessible by boat.

📍 100 km from MITK

🗿 Murudeshwar Temple

World's second-tallest Shiva statue overlooking the Arabian Sea — breathtaking at sunset.

📍 120 km from MITK

💧 Jog Falls

India's second-highest plunge waterfall — most magnificent during monsoon season.

📍 55 km from MITK

⛵ Malpe Beach

Beautiful beach near Udupi with boat rides to St. Mary's Island and fresh seafood.

📍 2 km from MITK

🌊 Kundapura Town

Charming coastal town with fresh seafood, local culture, and warm hospitality.

How to Reach

Getting to MITK

✈️ By Air

Nearest airports: Mangaluru International Airport (80 km) and Hubballi Airport (200 km). Taxis available from both airports.

🚆 By Train

Kundapura Railway Station is 5 km from MITK. Well connected to Mangaluru, Udupi, Mumbai, and Bengaluru.

🚌 By Bus

KSRTC and private buses from Bengaluru (8 hrs), Mangaluru (2 hrs), and Udupi (1 hr). Bus stand is 3 km from MITK.

🚗 By Road

NH-66 (Mumbai-Kanyakumari highway) passes through Kundapura. Well connected by road from all major Karnataka cities.
