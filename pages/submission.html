Submit Your Research

Original, unpublished papers in all areas of Computer Science & Engineering are welcome.

How to Submit

Submission Process

01

Prepare Your Manuscript

Format using the Elsevier Procedia template (MS Word or LaTeX). Papers must be 6–10 pages including all references.

02

Register on CMT Portal

Create an account at cmt3.research.microsoft.com — the official paper submission platform for ICETCSE-2026.

03

Submit & Get Confirmation

Upload your PDF, fill in paper metadata, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email with your paper ID.

04

Double-Blind Peer Review

Your paper is reviewed by 2–3 TPC members. Review results are communicated via email on the notification date.

05

Acceptance & Publication

Accepted papers require registration and in-person presentation at MITK. Papers published in Elsevier Procedia Computer Science.

📋 Submission Guidelines

  • Original, unpublished work only
  • Elsevier Procedia template required
  • 6–10 pages including references
  • Remove all author information (double-blind)
  • PDF format preferred (under 10 MB)
  • English language only
